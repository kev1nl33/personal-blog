The Senior "Repeater" Trap

Xu Feng, a 38-year-old Product Director, is exhausted. He's built his career on "experience": dissecting competitors, imitating features, and optimizing costs. But facing a saturated market, his CEO demands "disruptive innovation."


Xu Feng isn't lazy; he's trapped in a "Gravity Well of Experience." He isn't thinking; he's drawing parallels. [cite_start]He's essentially a highly skilled "human photocopier," creating slightly better versions of existing things. [cite: 1, 2]
