思维模型 No.009

无效勤奋
Ineffective Diligence

"你以为在冲刺，其实在绕圈。
破局之道是残忍舍弃。"

阅读时长: 12分钟 • 来源: 月见 • 2025-10-28

症状诊断

蒋涛的"陀螺人生"

38岁的蒋涛，外贸经理，为了对抗业绩下滑的焦虑，一年报了15门课。从AI文案到短视频逻辑，他的日程表塞满了"学习Sora"、"做B站"、"回复30封邮件"。

他每天忙到凌晨，以为在力挽狂澜，但在团队眼里却越来越"不靠谱"：方向一天三变，一会儿搞AIGC，一会儿搞出海调研。他陷在泥潭里，越挣扎下陷越快。他忙到窒息，却始终抓不到那根救命稻草。

核心病灶

机会囤积症：害怕错过，用战术上的"加法"掩盖战略上的"减法"。你不是在拓展，是在稀释。

认知操作系统 OS

加法思维 (Trap)

手电筒模式
缓解焦虑 = 虚假掌控 = 散射无力

减法思维 (Growth)

激光模式
残忍舍弃 = 聚焦 Top 5 = 穿透钢板

"你人生的'不做什么'，最终定义了你'能成为什么'。"

能量聚焦模型

为什么少即是多？

20件事每件5分，不如1件事100分。在这个时代，只有极致才能生存。

巴菲特"双清单"法则

List A

Top 5 (核心清单)

写下25个目标，圈出最重要的5个。这是你的灯塔，是你必须All in的"必然性"。

List B

Avoid (吸血鬼清单)

剩下的20个，不是"第二优先级"，而是"不惜一切代价避免"的吸血鬼。它们看起来很美，但会吸干你的精力。

Action

Kill (系统性拒绝)

对List B说"不"。每做一件List B的事，就是从List A偷走一块砖。必须像巴菲特一样残忍。

行动指南

从"光合作用"到"激光穿透"

01. 罗网式清点

拿出A3纸，写下所有想做的事。学AI、做B站、健身、学吉他... 像拉网一样把大脑清空。

02. 灵魂式圈选

只圈5个。问自己："哪件事做成了，能让其他事变得不重要？" "哪件事不做，我会死不瞑目？"

03. 决绝式拉黑

剩下的全部拉黑。蒋涛砍掉了AI课和无效会议，死磕A客户和东南亚市场。半年后，签下三年大单。

实战清单

TOOLKIT

"核心焦点"自检导航器

当你感到瞎忙、面临诱惑时，强制启动此程序。

1. 发散
清空大脑
"还有什么'可能'重要的？" 写下25+项愿望清单。
2. 聚焦
灵魂圈选
"哪5个能撬动全局？" 圈出List A (灯塔)。
3. 舍弃
决绝拉黑
剩下的全是List B (吸血鬼)。系统性拒绝。
4. 检视
每周校准
"精力是否90%投在List A？有没有对List B说不？"

"You think you're sprinting, but you're circling.

READ TIME: 12 MIN • SOURCE: YUE JIAN • 2025-10-28

THE DIAGNOSIS

Jiang's "Spinning Top" Life

Jiang Tao, 38, is a manager who signed up for 15 courses in a year to fight performance anxiety. From AI copywriting to short video logic, his schedule is packed with "Learn Sora", "Start Bilibili", "Reply to 30 emails".

He works until 2 AM, thinking he's saving the day, but his team sees him as "unreliable": changing directions daily. He is sinking in quicksand—the harder he struggles, the faster he sinks. He is suffocating from busyness but catching no straws.

Core Issue

Opportunity Hoarding Syndrome: Fear of missing out. Using tactical "Addition" to cover strategic "Subtraction".

MINDSET OS

Addition (Trap)

Flashlight Mode
Anxiety Relief = False Control = Scattered

Subtraction (Growth)

Laser Mode
Ruthless Elimination = Top 5 = Penetration

"What you DON'T do determines what you CAN become."

ENERGY MODEL

Why Less is More?

20 tasks at 5% effort < 1 task at 100% effort. Only excellence survives.

Buffett's "2-List" Rule

List A

Top 5 (Core List)

Write 25 goals. Circle the Top 5. These are your "Inevitabilities". Your Lighthouse.

List B

Avoid (Vampire List)

The other 20 are NOT "second priority". They are the "Avoid at All Costs" list. They suck your energy.

Action

Kill (Systematic Refusal)

Every time you do a List B item, you steal a brick from List A's castle. Be ruthless.

ACTION GUIDE

From "Photosynthesis" to "Laser Beam"

01. Net-Style Counting

Write everything down on A3 paper. Learning AI, Guitar, Gym... Empty your brain like dragging a net.

02. Soul-Style Selection

Circle only 5. Ask: "Which one makes others irrelevant?" "Which one defines me?"

03. Decisive Blacklisting

Block the rest. Jiang cut AI courses and meetings. He focused on Client A and SE Asia. 6 months later, he signed a 3-year deal.

CHECKLIST

TOOLKIT

"Core Focus" Navigator

Use this when you feel busy but unproductive.

1. Diverge
Empty Brain
"What 'might' be important?" Write 25+ wishes.
2. Focus
Soul Circle
"Which 5 leverage the whole game?" Circle List A.
3. Discard
Blacklist
The rest is List B (Vampires). Systematically refuse.
4. Review
Weekly Check
"Did 90% energy go to List A? Did I say NO to List B?"

"Your energy is like a match.
Bundle them to light a lighthouse for your life."
