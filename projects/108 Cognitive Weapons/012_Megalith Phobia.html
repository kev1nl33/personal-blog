思维模型 No.012

巨石恐惧症
Megalith Phobia

"你缺的不是执行力，
是拆解力。"

阅读时长: 12分钟 • 来源: 月见 • 2025-10-29

症状诊断

林涛的"PPT困境"

34岁的运营总监林涛，接手了S级项目"会员积分体系"。他开了两周"对齐会"，做出了50页精美PPT，获得高层掌声。但一个月过去了，项目纹丝不动。

技术等产品，产品等市场，市场等林涛。林涛每天忙于回复上百封邮件，处理部门拉扯，加班到凌晨。他像推着一块万吨巨石的西西弗斯，声势浩大，却原地踏步。他陷入了"巨石幻想"，以为靠协调和蛮力能推动大山，却不敢亲手把它砸碎。

核心病灶

巨石恐惧症：被目标的"宏大"和"模糊"绑架。宁愿忙于"表演执行"，也不愿做痛苦的"工程拆解"。

认知操作系统 OS

表演执行 (Trap)

迷恋模糊
战略宏大 = 安全感 = 虚假掌控

工程拆解 (Growth)

消灭模糊
战术具体 = 痛苦 = 真实推进

"你之所以焦虑，是因为你心里的那团任务是模糊的。WBS就是手术刀。"

拆解效应

颗粒度 vs 掌控感

任务越具体(颗粒度越细)，焦虑越低，掌控力越高。

WBS 三层下钻法

L1

顶层交付物 (Noun)

定义终局。不是动词"提升活跃"，而是名词"上线的积分体系V1.0"。如果定义不出来，活该你焦虑。

L2

关键子系统 (100%)

搭建骨架。必须遵循"100%原则"：子模块加起来不多不少等于L1。这是边界感，防止范围蔓延。

L3

可控工作包 (8/80)

填充血肉。拆解到"一个人"在"8-80小时"内能完成的独立任务。这是责任到人的最小砖块。

行动指南

从"总协调"到"总工程师"

01. 闭门画图

林涛停止了协调会。关起门，花一下午画出了L1-L3的树状图。从"务虚"转向"务实"。

02. 分发砖块

不再谈战略意义。把15个具体的"工作包"分发给具体的人。技术领走"接口"，产品领走"模型"。大家不再互相等待。

03. 掌控进度

PPT活了。林涛不再需要用权威压人，他只需要看着图追问交付。两个月后，项目如期上线。

实战清单

TOOLKIT

"巨石粉碎" WBS 导航器

将任何庞大任务转化为清晰的行动地图。

L1: 终局
定义交付物
"我最终要交付的那个名词是什么？" (e.g. 上线的网站)
L2: 骨架
关键子系统
"缺了哪块就不完整？" (e.g. 前端、后端、设计)
L3: 血肉
可控工作包
"1-2周内可完成的独立任务包。" (e.g. 首页UI)
L4: 责任
RACI矩阵
"谁负责？何时完？" (e.g. 张三 - 10月30日)

Mental Model 012

Megalith
Phobia

"You don't lack execution,
you lack Decomposition."

READ TIME: 12 MIN • SOURCE: YUE JIAN • 2025-10-29

THE DIAGNOSIS

Lin's "PPT Trap"

Lin Tao, 34, Ops Director. He took on an S-level project: "Membership System". He held "alignment meetings" for 2 weeks and made a beautiful 50-page PPT.

But one month later, zero progress. Tech waited for Product, Product waited for Market. Lin was busy replying to 100 emails and coordinating chaos. He was like Sisyphus pushing a giant rock, making noise but no movement. He fell into the "Megalith Fantasy": afraid to smash the rock, only daring to push it.

Core Issue

Megalith Phobia: Hijacked by the "grandeur" and "ambiguity" of the goal. Performing execution instead of engineering decomposition.

MINDSET OS

Performer (Trap)

Obsessed with Vague
Grand Strategy = False Safety

Engineer (Growth)

Kill Ambiguity
Concrete Tactics = Pain = Progress

"Your anxiety comes from the blurriness of the task. WBS is the scalpel to clear it."

DECOMPOSITION EFFECT

Granularity vs. Control

The finer the granularity, the lower the anxiety, and the higher the control.

WBS 3-Layer Drill

L1

Top Deliverable (Noun)

Define the End. Not "Improve Activity", but "Launched System V1.0". If you can't name it, you fail.

L2

Subsystems (100% Rule)

The Skeleton. Sub-modules must add up to exactly 100% of L1. This is the boundary to prevent scope creep.

L3

Work Packages (8/80)

The Flesh. Break down into tasks doable by one person in 8-80 hours. The smallest assignable brick.

ACTION GUIDE

From "Coordinator" to "Engineer"

01. Stop & Draw

Lin stopped meetings. He spent an afternoon drawing the L1-L3 tree. From "Vague" to "Concrete".

02. Distribute Bricks

No more strategy talk. 15 specific "packages" were assigned. Tech took "API", Product took "Model". No more waiting.

03. Control Progress

The PPT came alive. Lin didn't need authority; he chased deliverables. 2 months later, launched on time.

CHECKLIST

TOOLKIT

"Megalith Smasher" WBS

Turn any huge monster into a clear map.

L1: End
Define Outcome
"What is the final NOUN I am delivering?" (e.g. Launched Website)
L2: Skeleton
Subsystems
"What parts make the whole?" (e.g. Front, Back, Design)
L3: Flesh
Work Package
"Independent task for 1 person in 1-2 weeks." (e.g. Home UI)
L4: Owner
RACI
"Who does it? When?" (e.g. John - Oct 30)

"Don't push the megalith.
Break it down, and move the first pebble."
